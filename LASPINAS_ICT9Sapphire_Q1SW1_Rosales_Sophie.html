A Description List

Maria Clara
- A woman that is mestiza and plays a big role in the story, Noli Me Tangere. She is a beautiful and gentle woman, wife of Crisostomo Ibarra. Her full name is Maria Clara de los Santos and was raised by Capitan Tiago.
Capitan Tiago
- He is a wealthy businessman who owns many lands in San Diego,Laguna, and Pampanga. He has big donations in the Churches always. He is also the son of a wealthy businessman who owns a sugar company in Malabon. His wife is Pia Alba.
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalim
- He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra. He is described as a honest and joyful person. He studied in Spain for seven years and his learning skills expanded. Following his fathers death he came back to the Philippines.
